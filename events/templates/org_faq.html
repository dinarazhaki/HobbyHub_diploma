Frequently Asked Questions

What is HobbyHub?

HobbyHub is a platform where you can explore various activities, participate in challenges, and track your progress.

How do I participate in activities?

You can join activities by navigating to the 'Activities' section and selecting the one that interests you.

How is the leaderboard calculated?

The leaderboard ranks users based on their participation, completed challenges, and earned points.

How do I contact support?

You can contact support via the 'Contact Support' link in the footer or by emailing us at support@hobbyhub.com.
